FAQ

What is LingoJourney?

LingoJourney is an interactive language learning platform offering seminars, subscription-based lessons, and global community support to help learners achieve fluency.

Do I need any prior experience to join?

No, our programs are designed for all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced speakers. Each level is clearly labeled so you can start where you're comfortable.

How do I register for a seminar?

You can register through our Seminars page by selecting the city and date of your choice. Simply click "Register" and fill out the prompt.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

Yes, learners who complete a full course or attend a seminar receive a digital certificate of participation.
